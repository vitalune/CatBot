The Problem We Solved

Students often turn to AI for study help, but responses can be generic or inaccurate. AI tools like ChatGPT tend to act like you're a genius, when in reality, you're cramming for a midterm tomorrow and barely know the basics. Meanwhile, textbooks and class notes hold the right information, but they're not conversational or easy to search. Students needed an AI study buddy that actually understands their course material and meets them where they are.

When you need a study buddy who gets it.

What We Built

CatBot is a live, fully-functional AI tutor with the personality of a sarcastic cat who actually knows your course material.

CatBot is an AI chatbot powered by Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG) technology built with OpenAI's GPT-4o-mini, LlamaIndex, and LlamaParse. Unlike generic AI tools, CatBot ingests actual University of Pittsburgh course materials— syllabi, lecture slides, case studies, and practice problems—to provide accurate, course-specific answers.

Currently supporting CS1502 (Formal Methods), CS1530 (Software Engineering), LCJS1320 (Civil Rights Law), and MATH0280 (Linear Algebra), CatBot delivers personalized tutoring in an engaging, conversational way with cat puns.

Think of it as your patient tutor who never judges you for asking the same question seven times, wrapped in a furry package. And it's live right now on Hugging Face Spaces.

Key Features

  • Course-Specific Knowledge: Trained on 70%+ private Pitt course materials not available to ChatGPT
  • Academic Integrity: Guides learning with probing questions instead of providing direct homework answers
  • Red Team Tested: Passed 9/10 adversarial security tests (jailbreak resistant, privacy protected)
  • Accessible Design: Built with ADHD, visual, auditory, motor, and cognitive accessibility in mind
  • Free & Open Source: Available to all students on Hugging Face, code on GitHub
  • Sarcastic Cat Personality: Makes studying engaging with humor and cat puns
